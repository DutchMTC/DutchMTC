### 🌟 My Story
My journey into game development began as a kid, sparked by the creative possibilities of **Scratch**. That early fascination solidified my ambition, leading me straight to **Mediacollege Amsterdam** after middle school, eager to dive deep into the world I wanted to build.

Now, as a graduate, I've broadened my expertise to become a Full-Stack Developer. A pivotal moment was joining the **Unreal Academy** in Feb 2023. This year-long experience opened up the exciting realms of **Unreal Engine** and **Virtual Reality**, significantly shaping my skills.
